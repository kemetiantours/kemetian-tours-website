Good to Know

Everything you need to know before you travel

How do I book a tour package?

You can book through our website, by phone, or via WhatsApp. Select your package, provide traveler details, and make a payment to confirm.

How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking at least 2–3 months in advance to secure the best availability and rates.

Will I receive a confirmation after booking?

Yes — you'll receive a confirmation email with all travel details, including itinerary, payment receipt and contact information.

Can I book for a group?

Absolutely. We offer special rates and custom itineraries for group bookings — contact us for group travel arrangements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, bank transfers and online payment gateways. Some packages may allow installment plans.

Is my payment refundable if I cancel my trip?

Refund policies depend on the package. Some allow free cancellation within a certain period, while others may have a cancellation fee.

Are there additional fees beyond the package price?

Some tours may have optional add-ons, taxes or service fees. These are clearly stated before you confirm your booking.

What happens if my refund is delayed?

Refund processing times vary by payment method. If delayed, please reach out to customer service for an update.

Do I need a visa for my trip?

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. We can assist with visa applications if needed.

What travel documents should I bring?

Carry a valid passport, visa (if required), and printed copies of your booking confirmation.

Can you assist with flight bookings?

Yes — we offer flight booking services as part of our travel packages.

Will I have a tour guide during my trip?

Yes — most of our packages include professional local tour guides to enrich your experience.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

Inform us immediately and we'll adjust your itinerary accordingly.

Can I request special meals during the trip?

Absolutely — tell us your dietary preferences and we'll arrange suitable meals for you.

Is there 24/7 customer support while traveling?

Yes — we provide round-the-clock assistance for a seamless, stress-free journey.
