Cairo

4.8/5 (450+ reviews)

Cairo, the capital of Egypt, is a city of incredible history and vibrant culture. Home to some of the world's most iconic monuments, Cairo is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in ancient history.

Must-See Attractions:

  • Pyramids of Giza - The last remaining wonder of the ancient world
  • The Sphinx - Iconic limestone statue guarding the pyramids
  • Egyptian Museum - World's largest collection of ancient Egyptian artifacts
  • Khan el-Khalili Bazaar - Historic marketplace with traditional crafts
  • Citadel of Saladin - Medieval Islamic fortress with panoramic views
Best for: History enthusiasts, first-time visitors Best time: October - April Duration: 3-5 days

Luxor

4.9/5 (380+ reviews)

Ancient Thebes, now known as Luxor, is often called the world's greatest open-air museum. With temples, tombs, and artifacts spanning thousands of years, Luxor offers an unparalleled glimpse into pharaonic Egypt.

Must-See Attractions:

  • Valley of the Kings - Royal burial ground with hidden tombs
  • Karnak Temple - Massive ancient temple complex
  • Luxor Temple - Beautifully preserved temple on the Nile
  • Valley of the Queens - Tombs of royal wives and children
  • Colossi of Memnon - Iconic twin statues guarding the valley
Best for: History lovers, archaeology enthusiasts Best time: October - April Duration: 2-4 days
Book Luxor Tour

Aswan

4.7/5 (320+ reviews)

Aswan is the perfect destination for those seeking a more relaxed pace while still experiencing Egypt's wonders. With its picturesque Nile views and access to Nubian culture, Aswan offers a unique blend of history and natural beauty.

Must-See Attractions:

  • Aswan High Dam - Engineering marvel across the Nile
  • Philae Temple - Beautiful temple dedicated to Isis
  • Nubian Museum - Showcase of Nubian culture and heritage
  • Abu Simbel - Colossal temples carved into rock cliffs
  • Nile Felucca Cruise - Traditional sailing experience
Best for: Nature lovers, culture seekers Best time: October - April Duration: 2-3 days
